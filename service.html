What We Do?

Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much.

Healthy Food

Healthy food is essential for every child's survival, healthy food improves mental and physical ability of the children. Plan interntional helps to provide sufficient foods for the communities

Water & Sanitation

Water and sanitation are also essential for every child's survival. Plan international helps communites build school latrines and community water points. we also help establish organizations to ensure water points' continued management and maintenance.

Health Care

Access to health care is essential. Plan international helps communities build health clinic, train health care workers and invest in equpiment and medicine so children can grow up healthy and strong.

Quality Education

Education provides children with the knowledege and life skills to realize their full potential. plan international helps children acess quality education by training teachers, building new schools and breaking down barriers that prevent many children from attending and staying in school.

Child Participation

Child participation means that children are at the center of everything we do. Plan international helps children learn their rights and take active roles within their community. Child participation helps children engage in citizenship, express thier views and make decisions that will shape their future and influence the people around them.

Economic Security

Economic security means people have the skills and resilience to withstand hard economic times and grow their incomes. plan international works to overcome poverty by helping the communities around the world gain the financial security they need to thrive. we work with partners to provide skills training and education to help people secure a livelihood and support their families.
